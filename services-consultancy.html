Our Approach

Consultancy Built Around Your Goals, Not Ours

Scribble Green's consultancy practice brings research-grade rigour to strategic advisory. We don't deliver generic frameworks — we work alongside your team to diagnose real challenges and design bespoke responses grounded in evidence and built for execution.

How We Work

Our Engagement Process

Every consultancy engagement begins with genuine curiosity about your situation — and ends with a clear, deliverable outcome.

1

Discovery Call

We begin by understanding your goals, audience, constraints, and success criteria. No standard intake form — a focused conversation about what you actually need to achieve and by when.

2

Scoping & Proposal

A tailored proposal with clear deliverables, realistic timelines, and transparent pricing. No hidden costs, no scope creep. You know exactly what you're getting before we begin.

3

Research & Analysis

We dig into the real picture — desk research, stakeholder interviews, data analysis — whatever it takes to understand the landscape and your position in it without assumptions.

4

Strategy & Recommendations

Clear, prioritised recommendations presented as a strategy document, workshop, or advisory session — whatever format is most useful for your context and team.

5

Implementation Support

We don't disappear after the strategy deck. Depending on your needs, we can remain involved in execution — reviewing progress and keeping the work on track.
