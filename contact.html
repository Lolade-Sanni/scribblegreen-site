Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

What types of organisations do you typically work with? +

We work with a wide range, including NGOs and development organisations, private sector businesses, universities and research institutions, media houses, and individual professionals. If you have a writing, research, or digital communication challenge, we probably have experience in your sector.

What is your typical project turnaround time? +

It depends on the scope. A press release or short article can be turned around in 48–72 hours. A white paper or grant proposal typically takes 7–14 days. Larger research projects are scoped on a case-by-case basis. We always confirm timelines before work begins.

Do you work with clients outside Nigeria? +

Yes. While we are based in Lagos, we work with clients across Africa, the UK, Canada, and beyond. All our project engagements can be managed fully remotely, and our masterclasses and courses are delivered online to a global audience.

Can you run a workshop or training for our organisation? +

Absolutely. We design bespoke training programmes for teams and organisations across our areas of expertise — research methods, AI literacy, content strategy, academic writing, and more. Reach out with your requirements and we'll put together a proposal.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept bank transfer, Paystack (card payments), and Flutterwave. For international clients, we can arrange payment via wire transfer or platforms like Wise. Payment terms vary by project — we typically require a deposit before work begins.
