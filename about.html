How We Started

From a Single Idea to a Full-Service Firm

Scribble Green was founded in 2023 with a clear conviction: African businesses and researchers deserve the same quality of strategic communication support as their global counterparts.


What began as a boutique research and writing practice has grown into a multidisciplinary firm offering consultancy, AI-powered tools, professional training, and a growing library of digital resources — all rooted in rigorous methodology and editorial craft.


Today, we serve clients across academia, the private sector, NGOs, and media — helping them communicate with authority, clarity, and impact.
