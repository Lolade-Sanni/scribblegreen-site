Our Approach

AI Built With Purpose, Not Hype.

The AI landscape moves fast — and not everything that's new is useful. At Scribble Green, we cut through the noise to build digital tools and solutions that solve real problems: for researchers, writers, communicators, and the organisations they work within.

From AI-powered writing tools to bespoke web applications, our technical team builds with clarity of purpose, attention to user experience, and a deep understanding of the African digital context — including bandwidth constraints, regulatory realities, and workflow norms.

We also run AI literacy programmes that go beyond hype — helping teams develop confident, critical, and responsible relationships with the tools that are reshaping their industries.

Technology Services

What We Build

psychology

AI Writing & Content Tools

Custom AI-assisted writing tools designed for researchers, content teams, and organisations. Built for accuracy, voice preservation, and ethical AI use — not just speed. Includes AI humanisation services for content that needs to meet publication standards.

cloud

Cloud Solutions & Web Applications

Scalable, well-designed web applications tailored to your workflow — from client portals to research databases to internal productivity tools. We build for performance, maintainability, and the real conditions your users operate in.

api

Technical Documentation & APIs

Clear, comprehensive documentation for developers, technical teams, and end users — including API references, user guides, system architecture diagrams, and onboarding materials. We make complex systems accessible to the people who need to use them.

school

AI Literacy Training

Practical workshops and programmes that help professionals and academics use AI tools effectively, critically, and responsibly. Our flagship Notebook LM masterclass is the most popular example — but we design custom programmes for any AI toolset.

transform

Digital Transformation Advisory

Strategic guidance for organisations navigating digital change — from process automation to AI adoption roadmaps. We ground our advice in your operational realities, not generic best practices imported from markets with very different conditions.

verified

AI-Humanisation & Quality Review

Post-processing and editorial refinement of AI-generated content to meet quality, voice, and compliance standards for academic and professional publication. We ensure AI-assisted content reads as authored, not generated.
