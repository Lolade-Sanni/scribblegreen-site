Research & Writing

Research Associate

Open

Support client research projects across sectors including health, education, and technology. You'll conduct desk research, synthesise literature, and contribute to reports, white papers, and policy briefs under the guidance of our Lead Researcher.

Full-time / Part-time Remote Nigeria-based preferred
  • Strong academic writing skills across disciplines
  • Experience with literature review and synthesis
  • Familiarity with research databases (Google Scholar, JSTOR, etc.)
  • Ability to meet tight deadlines without sacrificing quality

Junior Web Developer

Open

Help build and maintain Scribble Green's web presence, internal tools, and client-facing digital products. You'll work closely with our Technical Lead on projects ranging from landing pages to AI-integrated web applications.

Part-time / Contract Remote
  • Solid understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Experience with at least one frontend framework (React preferred)
  • Keen eye for design and attention to detail
  • Comfortable working independently with minimal oversight

Marketing & Growth

Content & Social Media Coordinator

Open

Own Scribble Green's content calendar and social media presence. You'll create platform-native content, engage with our community, and work with the team to grow our audience across LinkedIn, Instagram, and X.

Part-time Remote Flexible hours
  • Strong written voice with range across formats and tones
  • Working knowledge of Instagram, LinkedIn, and X dynamics
  • Organised and consistent — you ship content on schedule
  • Genuine interest in research, writing, or AI a big plus

Training & Education

Masterclass Facilitator (Freelance)

Open

Facilitate live online masterclasses and workshops on behalf of Scribble Green, covering topics including research methods, academic writing, grant writing, and AI literacy. You'll deliver sessions designed by our curriculum team.

Freelance / Per Session Remote
  • Demonstrable expertise in at least one of our programme areas
  • Confident, engaging presenter — comfortable in live virtual environments
  • Flexible availability including weekends
  • University lecturing or training experience preferred
